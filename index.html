Our Popular Courses

Introduction to Economics

Economics is a wide-ranging and multifaceted subject that explores how individuals and societies choose to use scarce resources to satisfy their wants and needs. It focuses on the study of how economic agents interact with each other.

Biology

Biology is the scientific study of life and living organisms. At a university level, biology courses explore the organization, structure, function, growth, origin, and evolution of living things. It also covers topics such as biochemical processes, genetics, molecular and cell biology.

Computer Science

Computer Science is the study of computers and computational systems, including their design, analysis, implementation, and application. Computer science typically focuses on topics such as algorithms, data structures, databases, and operating systems.
